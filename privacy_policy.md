# Privacy Policy for Smart Health App

Last updated: March 25, 2026

## Overview

Smart Health App ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your health data.

## Data We Collect

Smart Health App accesses the following health data through Apple HealthKit and Apple Watch:

- Step count
- Active calories burned
- Heart rate
- Sleep analysis

This data is accessed solely to display your personal health trends and provide AI-powered insights within the App.

## How We Use Your Data

- To display your health metrics and trends
- To generate personalized AI health insights via Gemini AI
- To track progress toward your health goals

## Data Storage

All health data is read directly from Apple HealthKit and is not stored on external servers. Your data remains on your device and is governed by Apple's privacy framework.

## Third-Party Services

This App uses Google Gemini AI to generate health insights. Data sent to Gemini AI is used solely for generating insights and is not stored or shared.

## Your Rights

You can revoke the App's access to your health data at any time through your iPhone's Health app settings.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
